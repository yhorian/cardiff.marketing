Pug (formerly Jade) is a great templating language. While it's pipeline has been integrated with Eleventy there are some kinks to it. 
But don't panic! Once you're over these, there's nothing holding you back from enjoying the full benefits of both systems.

1. Getting Eleventy filters and shortcodes to work
2. Interpolation: !{ codes }, #{ codes }
